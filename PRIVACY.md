# Pile of Shame — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 18 June 2026_

Pile of Shame stores all of your data — your collection, photos, notes, and settings — locally on your device.

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking, and it does not require an account or an internet connection to work.

Any backups you create are files that you control and choose where to save; they are never sent to us or anyone else.

If you have questions about this policy, contact: danielpears31@gmail.com.
