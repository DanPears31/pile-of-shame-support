# Pile of Shame — Support

Pile of Shame is a miniature-collection tracker for tabletop hobbyists. Catalogue your minis, track build and paint progress, organise your boxes, and watch your pile of shame shrink.

## Need help?

For questions, bug reports, or feedback, email **danielpears31@gmail.com** and I'll get back to you.

When reporting a problem, it helps to include:
- Your device and iOS version
- What you were doing when it happened
- A screenshot, if you can

## Privacy

Pile of Shame keeps all your data on your device. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).
